CRACKING EGGS WITH A KNIFE

Cracking an egg with a knife, though less common than using a bowl, can be a precise and efficient method, especially for chefs who want to avoid shell fragments. By using the knife to create a clean break in the shell, you can minimize the chance of shell pieces ending up in the egg.

Method

1.Using one hand hold your egg with one side being revealed towards the ceiling.
 
2.Using your other hand tap the egg with the sharpish side of a butter knife.
 
3.Hold the egg over the bowl and pull the egg into two clean halves.
