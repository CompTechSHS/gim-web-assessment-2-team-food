CUTTING PIZZA WITH SCISSORS

Cutting pizza with scissors is a valid method, particularly for Roman-style pizza or when using scissors with a built-in spatula. It can be more efficient and preserve the crust better than using a pizza wheel, especially for thin, long pizzas or those on a pizza stone

Method

1.Using one hand in an oven mitt hold up one side of the pizza.
 
2.Using your other hand cut through the pizza using the scissors.
 
3.Repeat for as many slices as you like.
