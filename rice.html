Method/Ingredients

•1 cup rice
 
•2 teaspoons vegtable oil
 
•2 whisked eggs
 
2 strips of bacon chopped
 
•1 grated carrot
 
•1/2 cup frozen peas
 
•1 tablespoon soy sauce

1.Cook the rice in water for 12 minutes
 
2.Cook eggs in a pan and thickly chop them
 
3.Cook bacon for 4 minutes then add in the carrots
 
4.Add peas and rice
 
5.Cook for 4 minutes then add soy sauce and eggs
 
6.Take off heat and serve
