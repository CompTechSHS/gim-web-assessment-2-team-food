BANANA MEAT

Banana meat, a dish of fried bananas stuffed with pork, is a specialty of Dalaguete, Cebu, in the Philippines. The dish, also known as "Banana Meat," is a unique combination of sweet and savory flavors, featuring ripe saba bananas filled with ground pork, coated in a batter, and then fried until golden brown. It's a popular dish, especially during the town's fiesta celebration.
