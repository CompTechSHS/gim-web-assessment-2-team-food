Method/Ingredients

•1 cups peanut butter
 
•1 cup brown sugar
 
•1 egg

1.Preheat the oven to 180C
 
2.Mix the peanut butter, eggs, and sugar together in a bowl
 
3.Form balls of the mix with your hand and squish onto your baking paper lined tray
 
4.Put tray in oven and bake for 10 minutes
